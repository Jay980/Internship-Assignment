JavaScript Assignment No.6


Create three buttons with initial values "0" as text and one button as "Validate".

Requirement 1: On the click of any of the three buttons, value of that particular button should be increased by 1.

Requirement 2: On click of Validate button : (I) The value of unclicked buttons (among three buttons) should get increased by 1. (II) If all the three buttons are clicked, the value of oldest (first) clicked button (among three buttons) should get increased by 1.
